WHO WE ARE

Kinochronika is an independent film production company that focuses on character-driven, powerful documentaries and short narrative films. We work in all stages of the filmmaking process. We develop and produce independent films, commercials, and highlight reels.

It's amazing what goes into making something effortless.

Production has begun on a documentary about private Ballet School studio as young students, ages 14-18, who prepare for professional career in ballet. The instructor is a former principal ballerina of the Boston Ballet and now the owner of one of the top ballet schools in the country. The feature-length film will explore the lives of those ballet students who struggle through the pain, sweat, and tears to reach perfection within their art.
